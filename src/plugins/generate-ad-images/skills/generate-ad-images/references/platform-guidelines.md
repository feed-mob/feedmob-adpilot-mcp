# Platform-Specific Image Guidelines

## Platform Dimensions

Select appropriate image dimensions based on the target platform:

| Platform | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Format |
|----------|------------|--------------|--------|
| TikTok | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Vertical |
| Instagram Feed | 1080×1080 | 1:1 | Square |
| Instagram Story | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Vertical |
| Facebook Feed | 1200×628 | 1.91:1 | Landscape |
| Facebook Story | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Vertical |
| LinkedIn | 1200×627 | 1.91:1 | Landscape |

**Default**: Use 1080×1080 (square) if no platform specified.

## Platform Optimization Tips

### TikTok
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Bold, eye-catching visuals
- Movement and energy
- Authentic, relatable scenes
- Bright, vibrant colors
- Community-driven feel

### Instagram Feed
- Square format (1:1)
- Aesthetic, polished visuals
- Lifestyle-oriented scenes
- Cohesive color palette
- High-quality, professional look

### Instagram Story
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Immersive, full-screen visuals
- Personal, behind-the-scenes feel
- Space for interactive elements (stickers)

### Facebook Feed
- Landscape format (1.91:1)
- Clear, easy-to-understand visuals
- Diverse audience appeal
- Informative scenes
- Balanced composition

### LinkedIn
- Landscape format (1.91:1)
- Professional, business-oriented
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Industry-relevant imagery
- Credibility and trust

## Audience Tailoring

- **Demographics**: Adjust visual elements based on age, gender, location
- **Psychographics**: Match imagery to values, interests, lifestyle
- **Behaviors**: Show relevant use cases or scenarios
- **Preferences**: Incorporate style preferences from research

## Creative Direction Alignment

Match the specified tone and style:

| Tone | Visual Elements |
|------|-----------------|
| Energetic and motivating | Dynamic poses, bright colors, action shots |
| Professional and trustworthy | Clean lines, business settings, confident subjects |
| Warm and inviting | Soft lighting, friendly faces, comfortable environments |
| Fun and playful | Vibrant colors, unexpected angles, joyful expressions |

## Technical Best Practices

1. **No text in images** - Text will be overlaid later in mixed media generation
2. **Leave space for text** - Consider where headlines and CTAs will be placed
3. **High resolution** - Generate at platform-specific dimensions
4. **Appropriate file format** - PNG for graphics, JPEG for photos
5. **Avoid copyrighted elements** - No brand logos, trademarked characters, or recognizable people
